About this draft site

This site is a starter draft about storytelling as pedagogy in Canadian Indigenous ways of teaching and learning. It is designed so you can edit the wording, add course-specific references, and replace images later.

Important: Indigenous storytelling is diverse across Nations and communities. This draft does not speak for any Nation. Treat it as a structure you will refine with trusted sources and community guidance.

Site goals

  • Support reflective learning about storytelling and pedagogy.
  • Model respectful framing (protocols, positionality, and care with sources).
  • Offer adaptable early-years learning activity ideas.
