About (draft guide)

This page should briefly explain the purpose of the website and introduce the overall topic: storytelling as an Indigenous approach to teaching and learning.

Writing guidance: Keep this page short and introductory. Focus on explaining what the project is about, why the topic matters, and how the website is organized. Do not turn this page into a detailed literature review.

What this page should include

  • A brief explanation of the project topic and purpose.
  • A short note on why storytelling is significant in Indigenous teaching and learning.
  • A simple overview of the main sections of the website.
  • A respectful statement that this site is educational and does not represent any one Nation’s teachings.

Possible structure

  • Paragraph 1: What the website is about.
  • Paragraph 2: Why storytelling matters in this topic.
  • Paragraph 3: What readers will find in the rest of the site.

Suggested source type

Use 1–2 scholarly sources that help explain Indigenous storytelling, pedagogy, or respectful educational framing. Full citations can be placed on the References page instead of here.
